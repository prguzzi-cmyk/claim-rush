help_outline What this is
A built-in credit account every user on the platform has. You don't put real money in. The platform automatically adds credits when you hit milestones (signing a claim, closing a claim, recruiting someone, finishing training), and automatically takes credits out when you use platform tools (SMS, skip trace, AI calls).
lightbulb Why it exists
Two reasons: it rewards production without you having to write checks, and it controls cost by giving every user a budget for paid platform tools instead of letting anyone burn unlimited dollars on outreach.
