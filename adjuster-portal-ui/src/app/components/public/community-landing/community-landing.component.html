Already received a settlement check?

Settlement IQ — a line-by-line review of what your settlement should have included.

If your claim is already settled and the check has arrived, Settlement IQ reads the document line by line and identifies what's missing, what's underpriced, how depreciation was applied, and where policy provisions may have been misread. The same review a public adjuster would perform — typically returned in under 90 seconds.

  • Scope items the carrier may have omitted from your settlement
  • Unit pricing compared against regional norms for your county
  • Depreciation analysis — what was depreciated, whether it should have been
  • Estimated recovery range and statute-of-limitations window remaining
