Why a Public Adjuster

When a carrier settles a claim with a summary instead of a full line-item estimate, the homeowner is left without the line-by-line detail needed to verify scope, pricing, and applicable coverages. A public adjuster works for you — not the carrier — and is licensed to obtain the complete itemized estimate, review it against your policy and state law, and identify omissions or pricing gaps.

{{ tenantFirmName }} is {{ tenantFirmDescriptor }}. We routinely request full carrier estimates on behalf of homeowners who received only a settlement summary, then perform the line-item review the carrier didn't provide.

The initial consultation is free. We do not charge upfront fees; our compensation is a percentage of additional recovery we secure on your behalf, which means we only earn when you do.

If there is nothing to recover, we will tell you that directly and you will owe us nothing. A summary-only settlement does not always mean the carrier underpaid — it means we cannot tell from the summary alone, and the full estimate is the next honest step.
